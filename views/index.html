The Student Policy Initiative is a club at Greenwich High School that seeks to teach students to advocate for themselves more effectively. The club does this by engaging on the state and local level on issues that are important to its members.

To learn more about the club. visit the about page.
One of the projects the Student Policy Initiative has taken on is the Greenwich High School opportunity block. The members of this project have developed a detailed plan for their vision of the opportunity block and spoken in favor of it at the Board of Education, along with working with the GHS administration and student government to improve the opportunity block.
